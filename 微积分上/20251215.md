### 计算$\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n}x\,dx$

$$
\begin{gather}
t=\pi/2-x \\
\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n}x\,dx=\int_{0}^{\pi/2}cos^{n}x\,dx \\
-\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n-1}x\,dcosx \\
[-sin^{n-1}xcosx]|_{0}^{\pi/2}+(n-1)\int_{0}^{\pi/2}cos^{2}(x)(n-1)sin^{n-2}(x)\,dx \\
(n-1)\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n-2}(x)-sin^{n}x\,dx \\
得：\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n}x\,dx=\frac{n-1}{n}\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^{n-2}(x)\,dx \\
即:I_n=\frac{n-1}{n}I_{n-2} \\
而:I_1=\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sinx\,dx=1 \\
I_2=\int_{0}^{\pi/2}sin^2x\,dx=\pi/4 \\
最终有:I_n=\begin{cases} \frac {2m!!}{(2m+1)!!}\quad,n=2m+1 \\
\frac {(2m-1)!!}{2m!!}\frac{\pi}{2}\quad,n=2m \\
\end{cases}
\end{gather}
$$

### 通过函数特性在分部积分过程中消去项

eg:
$$
\frac{d}{dx}\int_{0}^{x}sin^{100}(x-t)\,dt \\
u=x-t \\
\frac{d}{dx}\int_{x}^{0}sin^{100}(u)\,d(x-u) \\
\frac{d}{dx}\int_{0}^{x}sin^{100}u\,du \\
原式等于:sin^{100}x \\ 
也可以使用Leibniz积分法则: \\
\frac{d}{dx} \left( \int_{a(x)}^{b(x)} f(x,t) \, dt \right) = f(x, b(x)) \cdot b'(x) - f(x, a(x)) \cdot a'(x) + \int_{a(x)}^{b(x)} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x,t) \, dt.
$$
